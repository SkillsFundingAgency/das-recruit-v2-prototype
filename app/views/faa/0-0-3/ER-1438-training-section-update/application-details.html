What you will do in your working day

As our HR Apprentice, example tasks will include:

  • Updating and maintaining HR databases and systems
  • Preparing contracts and letter
  • Organising exit interviews and probation meetings
  • Liaising with Payroll
  • Be the main contact for the HR inbox and respond to queries
  • Carrying out employee induction meetings
  • Assisting with the recruitment process of Industrial Placement and Apprentice Schemes
  • Supporting new starters when they join the business
  • Managing the Early Careers social media channels

The training you will be getting

As our apprentice you will undertake a Level 3 HR Support course at Bracknell & Wokingham College. We tailor training to the individual's development needs, so whether its Excel, company product knowledge, email best practice or something else, we will work with you to discover what you need.

What to expect at the end of your apprenticeship

Upon completion of the Level 3 HR Support course, we may be able to offer further training opportunities as an apprentice or if a permanent position is available then you could be considered for this opportunity.
